## Summary of Allegations and Investigations

-   Mail-in Ballots and Absentee Voting: Many allegations centered around the use of mail-in ballots and absentee voting, with claims of ballots being improperly counted, duplicated, or discarded.
-   Ballot Harvesting: Allegations of illegal ballot harvesting, where third parties collect and submit ballots on behalf of voters.
-   Voting Machine Irregularities: Claims that electronic voting machines were tampered with or programmed to alter vote counts.
-   Double Voting: Instances where individuals allegedly voted more than once, either in the same state or across different states.
-   Non-Citizen Voting: Allegations that non-citizens participated in the election.
-   Dead Voters: Claims that votes were cast in the names of deceased individuals.

## Outcomes and Legal Actions

-   Many of the allegations were investigated by state and federal authorities, resulting in a significant number of criminal convictions and civil penalties.
-   Courts across the country heard numerous lawsuits challenging the election results. The majority of these lawsuits were dismissed due to lack of evidence or standing.
-   Official audits and recounts in key battleground states confirmed the integrity of the election results, with no widespread fraud detected that could alter the outcome.
