That's a far more honest starting point. Most of us still want these things desperately. The Trappist isn't pretending otherwise; he's bringing that hunger to God and asking Him to change it.

These three prayers are, in my view, one of the most concise and searingly accurate diagnoses of the human ego ever written. We all want to be recognized. We all want to feel safe.
